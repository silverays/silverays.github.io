## Architecture

The platform was built on Laravel with a MySQL backend. The frontend used server-rendered templates with progressive JavaScript enhancement. Key features included:

- **Category-based browsing** with search and filters
- **User accounts** with listing management dashboards
- **Promoted listings** — a paid placement model for revenue
- **Messaging system** — buyer-seller communication without exposing email addresses

## Challenges

The classic two-sided marketplace problem: without listings, buyers wouldn't visit; without buyers, sellers wouldn't list. We addressed this by manually seeding initial listings in high-demand categories and running targeted social media campaigns in specific geographic areas.

## Lessons

Building a marketplace taught me that product is only a fraction of the equation. Operations — customer support, fraud detection, payment reconciliation — consumed far more effort than the software itself.
