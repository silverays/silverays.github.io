# Zetmel Brand Knowledge

## Brand name

**Zetmel**

## Founder

**Safraz Razik**

## Brand category

Recommended:

> AI-accelerated software engineering and product studio

Acceptable alternatives:

- AI software studio
- AI product engineering studio
- Independent software engineering studio
- Product development and modernization studio

Avoid presenting Zetmel as:

- A large full-service agency
- A pure AI research company
- A no-code automation shop
- A generic web-design agency
- A staffing company

## Brand essence

Zetmel combines Safraz's long-term engineering experience, founder mindset and current AI-assisted workflows to build products quickly and responsibly.

## Brand promise

> Move from idea, problem or unfinished product to reliable software with senior engineering ownership and AI-accelerated execution.

## Existing legacy brand assets

- **Tagline:** Innovation, Limitless.
- **Headline:** 15+ Years of Engineering Excellence.
- **Supporting line:** Expert full-stack development from custom compilers to global platforms.
- **Values:** Attractive, Efficient and Reliable.
- **Community terms used historically:** Zetner / Zet Partner; Zeteran

The new website may preserve “Innovation, Limitless.” as a secondary brand line. The primary homepage message should be more explicit about what Zetmel does.

## Updated positioning statement

> Zetmel is an AI-accelerated software engineering studio founded by Safraz Razik. It helps founders and product teams design, build, modernize and recover software with the speed of AI-assisted workflows and the judgment of 15+ years in engineering.

## Suggested tagline system

### Primary commercial tagline

> Senior engineering. AI-accelerated delivery.

### Supporting tagline

> From product idea to reliable software.

### Legacy-compatible line

> Innovation, Limitless—now accelerated by AI.

Use one primary tagline at a time. Do not stack all three in the hero.

## Values

### Attractive

Software should be visually considered, straightforward and inviting to use. UI quality is part of product quality.

### Efficient

Performance, productive workflows and clean implementation matter. Features should not require unnecessary complexity.

### Reliable

Software is a long-term commitment. Architecture, maintainability and engineering standards matter after launch.

### Responsible Acceleration

AI can shorten research and implementation cycles, but every important decision and output must remain under experienced human review.

### Ownership

Zetmel takes responsibility for understanding the problem, identifying risks and completing the work—not merely generating code.

## Audiences and messages

### Founders

**Need:** A senior technical partner who can turn a concept into a credible product.

**Message:** Work directly with a founder-level engineer who can shape architecture, implementation and delivery.

### Owners of unfinished products

**Need:** An MVP or AI-generated application that feels incomplete, fragile or visually raw.

**Message:** Zetmel audits, prioritizes and recovers products through structured technical and UX improvement sprints.

### Engineering teams

**Need:** Help with modernization, architecture, high-priority defects or code quality.

**Message:** Add experienced review, migration knowledge and hands-on implementation without adding management overhead.

### Recruiters and employers

**Need:** A senior React/full-stack engineer with ownership and current AI workflow experience.

**Message:** Safraz brings enterprise credibility, founder experience, open-source work and modern AI-assisted execution.

## Service descriptions

### AI-Accelerated Product Development

Design and build web applications through a structured, human-led process that uses AI for faster research, planning, implementation and review.

### Product Audit and Recovery

Assess an unfinished or unstable product, identify architectural and experience gaps, prioritize the work and execute focused recovery sprints.

### Frontend and Full-Stack Engineering

Build responsive product interfaces, backend integrations, APIs and data workflows using modern TypeScript, React, Next.js and supporting technologies.

### Legacy Modernization

Incrementally migrate or refactor established systems while protecting business continuity and reducing long-term technical risk.

### Technical Review and Architecture

Review code, architecture and development plans; uncover hidden risks; and establish a clearer path to production.

### Developer Tools and Custom Solutions

Create internal tools, libraries and specialist software when standard products do not fit the workflow.
