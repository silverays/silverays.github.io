## Recommended case-study format

Each case study should answer:

1. What was the product or business problem?
2. What constraints made it difficult?
3. What was Safraz responsible for?
4. What decisions did he make?
5. What was built or changed?
6. What measurable or observable result followed?
7. What did the work demonstrate?
8. Which details are confidential?
9. Which images or diagrams can be shown?
10. What is the direct next-step CTA?
