# Brand Voice, Messaging and SEO

## Voice

Write like an experienced engineer who can explain complexity clearly.

### The voice is

- Direct
- Precise
- Calm
- Confident
- Practical
- Thoughtful
- Product-aware
- Technically credible

### The voice is not

- Loud
- Hype-driven
- Buzzword-heavy
- Overly formal
- Self-congratulatory
- Vague agency language
- Artificially futuristic

## Sentence patterns

Prefer:

> I audit the product, identify the missing workflows and execute focused recovery sprints.

Avoid:

> We leverage cutting-edge, next-generation AI synergies to unlock transformative digital experiences.

Prefer:

> AI accelerates the implementation. Senior review protects the product.

Avoid:

> Harness the limitless power of AI to 10x your vision.

## Naming rules

- Write **Zetmel**, never “Zetemel.”
- Write **Safraz Razik** in full on first reference.
- Write **Motorola Solutions**, not simply Motorola, in formal case studies.
- Write **Oracle Commerce Cloud (OCC)** and **Oracle Storefront Framework (OSF)** on first reference.
- Write **Vue File Agent** with title capitalization.
- Write **AI-accelerated** or **AI-assisted** with a hyphen when used before a noun.
